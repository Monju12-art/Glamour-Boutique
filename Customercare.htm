Shipping & Returns

Shipping Information:

Delivery Time:

  • Standard shipping: 3-7 business days
  • Express shipping: 1-3 business days
  • International shipping: 7-14 business days (varies by location)

Shipping Fees:

  • Free shipping for orders over GHC 300
  • Flat rate for local deliveries: GHC 20
  • International shipping fees depend on weight and destination

Tracking: Once your order is shipped, you’ll receive a tracking link via email.

Returns Policy:

Eligibility:

  • Returns accepted within 30 days of delivery
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging
  • Sale items and accessories are final sale and non-returnable

Process:

  1. Email us at support@showstopper.com with your order number and reason for return
  2. Ship the item to: 123 Fashion Street, Accra, Ghana
  3. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after inspection

FAQ

How do I place an order?

Browse our collection, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, PayPal, and mobile money.

Can I change or cancel my order?

Changes or cancellations are only possible within 24 hours of placing the order. Contact us immediately.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries. Shipping fees and delivery times vary.

How can I contact customer support?

Email us at support@showstopper.com or call us at +233 123 456 789.
